The Seductive Story Behind LoveMoney!

LoveMoney! drops you into the shoes of a desperate soul, bound by a critical operation that demands $25,000 you can't afford. As you stagger from the hospital, fate leads you to a quaint booth where the charming Harvey Harvington awaits, promising dollars for your... attention. Desperation meets desire as your choices craft a narrative of closeness and consequence, weaving dark humor with intimate horror. The tale juggles urgency, whimsy, and moral quandaries, keeping you hooked in a realm where every caress carries weight. Its playful yet perilous allure turns sessions into a tantalizing tango of survival and seduction!

Gameplay: Touching Your Way Into Ethical Ecstasy

Core Interaction Rules

To savor LoveMoney!, grasp the flow of feelings and funds.

Your avatar grapples with a dire health scare, craving that $25,000 lifeline.

En route home in despair, Harvey's booth emerges with the enticing offer: "1 TOUCH = 1 DOLLAR."

Touching yields USD, piling up toward the $25,000 dream.

Escalate the intimacy to amp up earnings per touch, heightening the emotional and ethical heat.

As the tally climbs, drama and desire intensify, demanding savvy timing to juggle pace and passion.

All Intimate Tools in This Game

LoveMoney! unveils seven ways to connect with Harvey. Each ups the relational rush while spiking USD per touch, and each can be unlocked just once. They compel a clash between longing and limits, molding the play's pulse and the tale's tender twists.

Gentle Pet ($100): The softest start, sparking light emotional sparks with a modest gain.

Sweet Kiss ($500): Heightens the heart-flutter, accelerating rewards and a touch more turmoil.

Teasing Touch ($1,500): Brings bolder bonds, delivering a solid surge in USD per moment.

Passionate Embrace ($3,000): Deepens the drama, weighing desire against deepening doubts.

Fiery Caress ($6,000): Ignites intense intrigue, boosting bounties and budding burdens.

Sensual Stroke ($10,000): Pushes profound passions, nearing the edge of ecstasy and excess.

Ultimate Union ($20,000): The pinnacle of proximity, maxing out monetary and moral mayhem.

Effortless Control Mechanics

Left Click: Sparks a dollar through tender interaction with Harvey Harvington.

Intimacy Slider: Elevates earnings per touch while amplifying affection's aftershocks.

Pause Button: Gives space to reflect without the rush of romance.

Streamlined controls render LoveMoney! inviting yet intricately intense, morphing easy gestures into a soul-stirring saga. The ease spotlights strategy, suspense, and the sly sensuality woven into every exchange.
